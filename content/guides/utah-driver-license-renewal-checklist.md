## Before you start

A checklist can prevent delays during a Utah driver license renewal.

## Renewal checklist

- Confirm your license is not suspended.
- Check online renewal eligibility.
- Make sure your mailing address is current.
- Prepare identity documents if a REAL ID step is needed.
- Save confirmation after payment.
