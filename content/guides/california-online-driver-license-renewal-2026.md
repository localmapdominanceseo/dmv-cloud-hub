## Short answer

Many California drivers can renew online, but not every driver qualifies.

## Online renewal is usually easier when

- Your address is current.
- Your identity status does not need review.
- Your photo or vision screening is not due.
- Your record has no block.

## You may need an office visit if

- Your notice says an in-person visit is required.
- Your identity documents need review.
- Your photo must be updated.
