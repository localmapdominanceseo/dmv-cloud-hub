## When this guide is useful

Use this guide if your Wyoming driver's license was lost, stolen, damaged, or never arrived by mail.

## Fast replacement path

1. Check whether your personal details are still current.
2. Use the official Wyoming DOT service if you are eligible online.
3. Visit a local driver services office if your name, address, or identity record needs review.
4. Keep the receipt or temporary credential until the replacement arrives.

## What to prepare

- Your legal name
- Date of birth
- Current Wyoming mailing address
- License number if available
- Payment method for the official replacement fee
