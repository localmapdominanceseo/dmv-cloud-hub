## Why fees vary

Florida driver license costs can vary depending on the transaction type.

## Common fee situations

- Standard renewal.
- Replacement after loss or damage.
- REAL ID update.
- Address correction.
- Late or expired license situations.

## Best practice

Confirm the official fee before paying.
