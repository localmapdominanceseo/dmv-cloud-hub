## Main idea

Texas REAL ID usually requires proof of identity, Social Security number, Texas residency, and lawful status where applicable.

## Document groups

### Identity

Bring an accepted identity document such as a passport or birth certificate.

### Social Security

Prepare a Social Security card, W-2, pay stub, or accepted alternative if allowed.

### Texas residency

Most applicants need two proofs of Texas residency.
