## Why this checklist matters

Texas REAL ID applications can be delayed when a document does not match your legal name, current address, or identity record.

## Core document groups

### Identity

Bring an accepted identity document such as a passport, birth certificate, or other approved record.

### Social Security

Prepare a Social Security card, W-2, pay stub, or accepted alternative if allowed.

### Texas residency

Most applicants should prepare two documents showing current Texas residency.

## Common mistakes

- Bringing documents with an old address.
- Forgetting name-change records.
- Assuming photocopies are enough.
- Not checking the official DPS list first.
