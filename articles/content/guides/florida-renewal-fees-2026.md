## Why fees vary

Florida driver license costs can vary based on renewal type, replacement needs, endorsements, and whether other corrections are included.

## Common fee situations

- Standard renewal
- Replacement after loss or damage
- REAL ID update
- Address correction
- Late or expired license situations

## Best practice

Check the official FLHSMV page before paying. Fees can change and some transactions may include extra service charges.
