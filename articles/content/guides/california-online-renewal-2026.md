## Short answer

Many California drivers can renew online, but not every renewal is eligible.

## You may need an office visit if

- Your notice says an in-person visit is required.
- Your identity documents need review.
- Your photo or vision screening must be updated.
- Your driving record has an issue that blocks online renewal.

## Best renewal order

1. Check your renewal notice.
2. Try the official DMV online renewal system.
3. Prepare documents if the system sends you to an office.
