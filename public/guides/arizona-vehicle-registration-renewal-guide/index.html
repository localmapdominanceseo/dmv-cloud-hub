Arizona Vehicle Registration Renewal Guide

Arizona registration renewal options, emissions notes, timing, and official MVD resources.

What this covers

This guide is for Arizona vehicle registration renewal, not driver license renewal.

Renewal steps

  1. Check your renewal notice.
  2. Complete emissions testing if required.
  3. Renew through the official Arizona MVD service.
  4. Save your confirmation.
