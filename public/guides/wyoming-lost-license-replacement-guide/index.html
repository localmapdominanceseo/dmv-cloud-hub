Wyoming Lost License Replacement Guide

How Wyoming drivers can prepare to replace a lost, stolen, damaged, or never-received license.

When this guide is useful

Use this guide if your Wyoming driver license was lost, stolen, damaged, or did not arrive.

Replacement path

  1. Confirm your personal information is current.
  2. Check whether online replacement is available.
  3. Prepare identity details and payment.
  4. Visit an office if your record needs review.
