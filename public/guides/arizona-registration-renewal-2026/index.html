Arizona Vehicle Registration Renewal Guide for 2026

Arizona registration renewal options, timing, emissions notes, and official MVD links.

What this covers

This guide is for Arizona drivers renewing vehicle registration, not a driver license.

Renewal steps

  1. Check your renewal notice.
  2. Complete emissions testing if required in your area.
  3. Renew through the official Arizona MVD service.
  4. Save your confirmation and wait for updated registration materials.
