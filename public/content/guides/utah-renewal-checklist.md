## Before you renew

This checklist helps Utah drivers avoid delays before starting a renewal.

## Renewal checklist

- Confirm your license has not been suspended.
- Check whether you are eligible for online renewal.
- Make sure your mailing address is correct.
- Prepare identity documents if REAL ID action is required.
- Review the official Utah Driver License Division instructions.

## Online renewal notes

Online renewal is usually the fastest route when the driver record does not require an in-person update.
